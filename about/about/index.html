Biography

Chathuranga Rajapaksha is a graduate of University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka. His research interests include Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Deep Learning. She leads the Robotic Neurobiology group, which develops self-reconfiguring robots, systems of self-organizing robots, and mobile sensor networks.
